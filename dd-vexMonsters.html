Vex Monsters
Last Updated: August 18, 2022

Vex Monsters is a fantasy 2D platforming game, where you save monsters from an evil mysterious villain.

This is the map, where the game take place.

It has been two years since we started this project. There is an entire level to play on with trees, monsters, enemies, platforms in trees, caverns and much more. The objective is to save the monsters in the cages throughout the level from enemies. They will follow you back to home and help you on your conquest. Once at their home, the mother of the monsters you saved will attack you, enraged because it got possessed by a dark spirit. She becomes much bigger than she usually is and has purple spikes. Once you defeat it, some of the monsters you saved will join your party while others stay behind with their mother.

This is a sneak peek of the level.

Before you begin the first level, there is a place where you can gear up on supplies and learn how to play (move, jump, shoot a gun, etc.) and learn what your mission and what the game is about via intro cutscene.
