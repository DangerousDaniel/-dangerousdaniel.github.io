Android Development

In my Android development class, I built a Quiz Builder, where you could enter your name, play a quiz with questions, answers, and track your score. It had up to 4 questions with 4 multiple choices to choose your answers. It had error checking for if you didnâ€™t enter a name or choose the right answer for the questions. When you reach the end, it tells you your full score and asks if you want to play again.

Before I created the app, I built a couple of storyboards that outline how it works.

On the first screen, it will ask for your name. Once you enter your name, you press the button down to continue and start the quiz.

The next few pages were pages of questions. There was a list of the possible answers, and the questions.

If you got the question right it was programmed to turn the selection green and if you got it wrong it turned red.

The last page told you your overall score, and asked if you want to try again. If the player selected try again, the app will reset back to the beginning.
