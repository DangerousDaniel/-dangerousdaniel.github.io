My Unity Sandbox 2020

This sandbox is where I play and learn new things about Unity. For example, one day, I needed a dialogue box for my 2D platform game named Vex Monsters. I looked up a video from Brackeys, follow it and implement it into the sandbox. The person I watch video is a professional Unity development and create fantastic unity tutorials for all levels. I was able to follow it with no problem.

In the end, I created a dialogue box that when the player walked up to it. The box will pop up with their name and message. This was made in my sandbox, and I created one for Vex monsters.
